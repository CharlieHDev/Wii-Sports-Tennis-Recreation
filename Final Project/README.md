# Final Project Documentation

## Ideation
The idea for this project is to create a game similar to Wii Sports tennis, a two player game of tennis involving the use of gloves to replicate tennis rackets. Via the users movement inputs, they would be able to swing their hand and imitate hitting a tennis ball across a court.

The tennis ball would be displayed on a Liquid Crystal Display (LCD) screen, the ball would be creating using ASCII art or even simple text displayed in a way that replicates a ball bouncing from one side of the screen to the other. The player would have to hit the ball using their glove movement input before the ball reaches the end of their side of the screen.

Inspiration from this idea came from my experimentation with accelerometers and their uses. The idea came to mind that I could replicate Wii Sports Tennis but a simpler version, creating gloves which could act as the Wii Remotes, with a central device for receiving the information and displaying the game on a screen.

The intended audience for this project are people who enjoy video games, I want to create an enjoyable experience similar to the experience provided by the Nintendo Wii, an experience for families and friends to enjoy together and for people of all ages.
